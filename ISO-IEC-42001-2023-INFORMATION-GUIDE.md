## Standard Overview

### What is ISO/IEC 42001?

ISO/IEC 42001 is the **world's first international management system standard for artificial intelligence**. It specifies requirements for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving an Artificial Intelligence Management System (AIMS) within organizations.

### Scope

**Applies to:**
- Organizations developing AI systems
- Organizations providing AI products/services
- Organizations using AI systems
- Any size, any industry, public or private sector

**Covers:**
- Responsible development of AI
- Ethical use of AI
- AI risk management
- AI governance framework

## Key Requirements Summary

### 1. AI Risk Management

**Required:**
- Risk assessment process
- Risk treatment plans
- Regular risk reviews
- Documentation of risks

**AI-Specific Risks to Address:**
- Algorithmic bias
- Lack of transparency
- Security vulnerabilities
- Privacy impacts
- Safety concerns
- Ethical issues

### 2. AI Impact Assessment

**Required for high-risk AI:**
- Systematic impact assessment
- Stakeholder consultation
- Documentation of impacts
- Mitigation measures

- Societal impacts
- Environmental impacts
- Economic impacts

## Implementation Approach

### Phase 1: Preparation (Months 1-2)

1. **Obtain Standard**
   - Purchase from ISO or authorized distributor
   - Review entire document
   - Identify applicable requirements

2. **Gap Analysis**
   - Compare current practices to ISO 42001
   - Identify gaps
   - Prioritize actions

3. **Leadership Commitment**
   - Secure executive sponsorship
   - Allocate resources
   - Establish governance structure

### Phase 2: Design (Months 3-4)

4. **AI System Inventory**
   - Catalog all AI systems
   - Classify by risk level
   - Document current state

5. **Risk Assessment**
   - Develop methodology
   - Assess each AI system
   - Document risks and treatments

6. **Policy Development**
   - AI management policy
   - Supporting procedures
   - Documentation templates

### Phase 3: Implementation (Months 5-8)

7. **Process Implementation**
   - Impact assessment process
   - Life cycle controls
   - Monitoring procedures

8. **Competence Development**
   - Training programs
   - Awareness campaigns
   - Skill development

9. **Documentation**
   - AIMS documentation
   - Records and evidence
   - Knowledge base

### Phase 4: Evaluation (Months 9-10)

10. **Internal Audit**
    - Audit AIMS against standard
    - Identify nonconformities
    - Corrective actions

11. **Management Review**
    - Review AIMS effectiveness
    - Identify improvements
    - Resource allocation

### Phase 5: Certification (Optional) (Months 11-12)

12. **Certification Audit**
    - Select certification body
    - Stage 1 audit (documentation)
    - Stage 2 audit (implementation)
    - Address findings

13. **Surveillance**
    - Annual surveillance audits
    - Continual improvement
    - Recertification (every 3 years)

## Certification Process

### Certification Bodies

**Major Certification Bodies:**
- BSI (British Standards Institution)
- SGS
- Bureau Veritas
- DNV
- TÜV SÜD
- Intertek

### Certification Steps

1. **Application**
   - Submit application to certification body
   - Define scope of certification
   - Agree on audit schedule

2. **Stage 1 Audit (Documentation Review)**
   - Review AIMS documentation
   - Assess readiness
   - Identify gaps
   - Duration: 1-2 days

3. **Stage 2 Audit (Implementation Audit)**
   - On-site assessment
   - Interview personnel
   - Review records
   - Observe processes
   - Duration: 2-5 days

4. **Certification Decision**
   - Review audit findings
   - Address nonconformities
   - Issue certificate (if compliant)

5. **Surveillance Audits**
   - Annual audits
   - Sample of AI systems
   - Continual improvement review

6. **Recertification**
   - Every 3 years
   - Full audit
   - Reissue certificate

### Certification Costs

**Estimated Costs:**
- Initial certification: $15,000 - $50,000
- Annual surveillance: $5,000 - $15,000
- Recertification: $10,000 - $30,000

*Varies by organization size, scope, and certification body*
